Darumaban (an LD31 Jam Entry)

How to Play

  • Press [Arrow keys] to move
  • Hold [Space] while moving to roll a snowball
  • Roll a snowball over snow to increase its size
  • Rolling 3 snowballs of decreasing size on top of each other makes a snowman
  • The goal is to fill all the marked tiles with snowmen
  • Thanks for playing, and have fun!
