Darumaban (Postjam version) (Source code)

How to Play

  • Press [Arrow keys] or [WASD] to move
  • Hold [Space] or [Shift] while moving to roll a snowball
  • Hold [Enter] to see where snowballs can be rolled from the current position
  • Press [P] to switch movement mode between continuous and move-one-tile-per-key-press
  • Press [Escape] to restart the level
  • Roll a snowball over snow to increase its size
  • Rolling 3 snowballs of decreasing sizes on top of each other makes a snowman
  • The goal is to fill all the marked tiles with snowmen
  • Thanks for playing, and have fun!
